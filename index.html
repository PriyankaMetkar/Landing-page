ABOUT

We are fortunate to share the land, water and sky with an impressive diversity of wildlife. To enable people and wildlife to share lanscapes. We equip people with innovative science-based tools and stratagies to reduce conflicts that may occur with wildlife. Our goal is to foster transformation in both human attitudes and how wildlife and people interact, from conflict to coexistence.
